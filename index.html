Soups That Rock

Ladle of Love: Our signature soup.

A hearty white bean stew with fire-roasted tomatoes and fresh spinach. Vegan. Gluten free. *** Each quart of Ladle of Love sold means Rock the Ladle makes a donation to Interfaith Hospitality Network.

All-day Tomato Basil: Full of organic vegetables

Fresh basil, and real cream, this soup is simmered all day to bring out a delightful array of both bold and subtle flavors. Vegetarian. Gluten free.

Grandma's Vegetable Lentil:

This isn't a time warp! We've added vibrant seasonings and hearty legumes to Grandma's secret recipe. Vegan. Gluten free.

Tim’s Gone Nuts:

A curried cauliflower soup made creamy with cashews and coconut milk. Savory and bold. Vegan. Gluten free.

About the SoupMaster:

Chef: Amy Fletcher

Our chef and founder is a mother of four pursuing her dream of getting delicious, all-natural gourmet soups in the hands (and bellies!) of our wonderful and diverse community. Amy has extensive experience preparing fabulous, nutritious vegetarian/vegan, gluten free foods. She is excited to collaborate with farmers and merchants in and around the Queen City.
