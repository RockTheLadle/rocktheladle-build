About the SoupMaster

Amy Fletcher

Rock the Ladle's founder is a mother of four pursuing her dream of putting delicious, all-natural gourmet soups in the hands (and bellies!) of our wonderful and diverse community. Amy has extensive experience preparing fabulous, nutritious vegetarian/vegan gluten-free foods. She is excited to collaborate with farmers and merchants in and around the Queen City. Amy's passion for cooking and penchant for creating mouthwatering soups the whole family can enjoy led to friends and other loved ones encouraging her to start her own food-based business for some 20 years now. Finally taking the leap, she is now delighted to make these lovingly homemade soups available to YOUR family.
